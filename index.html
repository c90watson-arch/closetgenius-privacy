Privacy Policy — ClosetGenius

Last updated: 26 October 2025

Welcome to ClosetGenius (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Note: This version reflects the current App behavior where all data is stored locally on your device and no account sign-in is required. If we add cloud sync, analytics, or third-party integrations later, this Policy will be updated.

1. Who we are

Controller: ClosetGenius (“we”, “us”).
Contact: support@closetgenius.app

2. What data we collect

Data you provide: Closet items, preferences, and style feedback.

Automatically collected: Basic device state stored locally; HTTPS network requests for weather or marketplace links.

No sensitive data: Approximate location may be used for weather only if enabled.

3. Where your data lives

Your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all stored data. If cloud backup features are added, this policy will be updated.

4. How we use data

  • Provide outfit suggestions based on your wardrobe and preferences
  • Suggest resale prices for items you choose to sell
  • Improve recommendations from your feedback

5. Sharing of data

We do not share your personal data. When you choose to post on external marketplaces (Vinted, Depop, eBay), you use those platforms under their own policies.

6. Legal basis (EEA/UK)

We process your data under legitimate interests to provide and improve the App, and consent for optional permissions (e.g., location).

7. Your rights

You may reset or delete your local data anytime via the in-app option or by uninstalling. For questions, email support@closetgenius.app.

8. Retention & Security

Data stays on your device until deleted. All network communication uses HTTPS for security.

9. Children

ClosetGenius is not directed at children under 13 years old.

10. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Updates will appear here with a new “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

Email: support@closetgenius.app
Developer: c90watson-arch

  • Data shared: None
